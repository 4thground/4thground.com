At 4TH GROUND, we are passionate about bringing independent films to audiences around the world. We work closely with filmmakers, producers, and production companies to help their projects reach the global market through strategic digital distribution.

Our mission is to empower independent filmmakers by creating opportunities for their films to be seen from medium-sized to prominent streaming platforms worldwide. We believe great cinema deserves visibility, regardless of budget or origin.

With a filmmaker-first approach, we focus on building meaningful partnerships, supporting the creatives, and helping quality films expand beyond borders. From emerging filmmakers to established production companies, we are committed to delivering content to platforms that connect stories with audiences everywhere.
